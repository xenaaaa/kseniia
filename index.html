About Me

Hello world! I’m Ksenia, I am 15 years old and I am from a small town in the South of Ukraine. I am passionate about science: I am a prize-winner and participant of various tournaments, Olympiads in physics, astronomy, and programming. I am the owner of 3rd place in the All-Ukrainian Tournament of Young Physics and was the toungest on the selections for International Astronomy ans Astrophysics Olympiad. In STEM I mostly like things involving non-standard approaches. In physics I am passionate about complex topics like Quantum Mechanics and Fluid Dynamics; in math - about Topology; in programming - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Also, I am interested in various research works. For example, at the moment I am writing work on theoretical astrophysics, namely "Research of Pulsing Stars", and I am a winner of numerous hackathons that involved research and real-life problem-solving work.

A big part of my life is popularazing science: for me, learning is also an opportunity to make lifelong friendships, share my values and resources. I volunteered in Mavka - a startup that helps Ukrainian schoolchildren prepare for the final exams for free. I devised the solutions and explanations for final high school exam in physics ZNO - similar to SAT 2 Physics (100 hours of volunteering). Also, I am a member of UkrTeenScience and write weekly articles about different physics pehomenas. Additionally, I am passionate about art and music. I have been singing and dancing professionally since I was 7 years old, and I am a prize-winner in multiple national art competitions as well as participated at International music exhange program. I also play piano.

For fun, I play chess and checkers (Nationaly recognized player), and participate in Intelectual games, like What?Where?When? (1st place on National tournament); I also adore listening to podcasts on Artificial Intelligence and cooking.
