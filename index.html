About Me

My name is Ksenia, I am 15 years old and I am from a small town in the South of Ukraine. I am interested in science, namely physics, astronomy, mathematics and programming. I like complex and non-standard topics in sciences like Topology, Quantum Mechanics, Fluid Dynamics, Artificial Intelligence and Cryptography. I like to look for non-standard ideas when solving problems.

Besides science, I enjoy drawing and making music. I paint with watercolors and oil paints, and also sing and play on the piano. Also, I enjoy playing mind games such as "What? Where? When?" and different quizzes.
